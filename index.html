Bolton Food and Drink Festival

Drop into Bolton this August Bank Holiday Weekend for a taste of the North West's biggest and best food and drink festival. With cookery demos from some of the best chefs around, almost 200 food, drink, arts and crafts traders, special events, great family friendly entertainment, world class street theatre and loads of live music, its not to be missed! For more info and to book tickets go to www.boltonfoodanddrinkfestival.com!

Ironman - Bolton

Experience the fantastic atmosphere and support the awe inspiring athletes in their quest to become an 'Ironman'! The breathtaking course consists of a 2.4 mile, a 112 mile bicycle ride and a marathon 26.22 mile run, raced in that order without a break. Its a spectator friendly course and you can watch the athletes swim at Pennington Flash from 6.30am, cycle around Bolton from 10am and finish with the marathon on Le Mans Crescent from 2pm onwards.
